EC3

The Electronic Communications and Computer Club (IT Club)

Clubs & Societies

Back to Clubs & Societies

We pride ourselves to be one of the very few Infocomm clubs in Singapore to employ a senior-junior teaching model without external trainers that have not only raised the standards of our members but also fostered long-lasting bonds between the seniors and juniors. In EC³, members are given the freedom and space to pursue their own interest in the different aspects of IT, while receiving ample competition opportunities throughout the year, all in a family-like environment.
