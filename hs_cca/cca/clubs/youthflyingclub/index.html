Youth Flying Club

Back to Clubs & Societies

The Hwachong Youth Flying Club was established in HCI in the year 2004, as a second EP3, under the umbrella of the Singapore Youth Flying Club. Since its formation, it has always been actively empowering individuals who have a deep passion for flying to pursue their interests, opening a gateway to learning about man's successful conquest over the skies, and in turn rewarding them with the chance of achieving the PPL in year 5 and 6 — Private pilot's license in short — A prestigious award given to only those deserving candidates after intense rounds of interviews and tests. With this license at hand, one can then be considered a true 'pilot'. At the secondary level, YFC serves to introduce all members to aviation, and prepares members with a deep passion for flight by inculcating them with a strong knowledge base, through camps, interactive lessons, normal flight trainings, simulation trainings, and joy rides on various military aircraft-all of which gives a strong headstart when applying for a future career as a pilot. Apart from flying, SYFC is also considered a Uniformed Group which allows members to develop a strong sense of discipline, teamwork and leadership.
