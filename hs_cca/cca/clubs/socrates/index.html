Socrates

Clubs & Societies

Back to Clubs & Societies

Welcome to the Socrates Club, a club for those who are enthusiastic in Mathematics and wish to take it further from the classroom. Sessions are held once a week on Friday afternoons and you can look forward to fun Maths sessions that will broaden your knowledge and skill as well as board game sessions at the end just to relax and have fun with your fellow members. We do not forget the balance between the mind and the body so team sports sessions like soccer are also organized frequently for members to both enjoy and forge greater bonds with other members. Organisations of events, including Maths Fun Rooms @ HCI Open House, Maths Camps for P6 students, Maths Enrichment Workshop for primary school students and Singapore Maths Project Festival (SMPF Prelim @ HCI), give plenty of leadership opportunities to the members. These also provide chances for the members to serve and contribute to the school and community.

Our club has achieved numerous awards, with many gold and silver medals in the Singapore Mathematical Olympiad, various perfect scorers in the American Mathematics Competition, and even a gold medal in the International Mathematics Competition! So come enter the world of Mathematics and reach for greater heights with us, the Socrates Club!
