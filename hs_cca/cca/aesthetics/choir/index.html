Choir

The Hwa Chong Choir was founded in 2003 as the Vocal Ensemble by a group of students who were deeply passionate about singing. Though it had a small membership, the Vocal Ensemble left a deep impression on the school populace by performing at various events. Since then, it has grown to our present size of about 40 members. The HCI High School Choir today is definitely one of the most vibrant and interesting EP3s within Hwa Chong. With a large repertoire of many songs spanning different genres, it has performed at several events, such as the HCJC Choir's concert as well as festive celebrations and school assemblies. We also frequently collaborate with other choirs, be it choral workshops with River Valley High and CHIJ St Joseph Institution, or guest performing at the Nanyang Girls' High School Choir concert. In 2009, the choir went on their first overseas CIP trip in Vietnam. They brought their voices as blessings to many. At the end of every year, they would sing for Hospitals and Homes. On the Awards front, the Choir has achieved a Merit Award at the prestigious ABRSM Examination 2006 and GOLD Awards for the Singapore Youth Festival Competitions in 2007 and 2011. The future certainly looks bright for it!
