PE and CCA

Piano Ensemble

Aesthetics

Back to Aesthetics

Debuted 2 years ago and comprising 8 first CCA members who are specially selected for their talent and passion in the pianoforte, we host the periodic Piano Masterclasses with local and international pianists such as Ms Chisato Kusunoki, Piano Instructors from the NUS Conservatory of Music and Mr Ong Lip Tat. Our repertoire stretches from the Baroque Era with Bach's Fugues and Inventions, the Classical Roccoco, the Romantic's Chopin and Debussy as well as downright Contemporary pieces. In addition, we hold music composition workshops and music therapy sessions at hospices for the old and infirm. Our boys also prepare for ABRSM's theory examinations in their weekly theory classes. At the Old Parliament House, in 2011, we hold our first concert, which was supported by gifted pianists from other schools including Dunman High and National Junior College. In the pipeline, in 2012, we plan to produce Hwa Chong's very first piano music album collection.

What we do

Play pieces in our repertoire for analysis and critique by Mr Tan and other CCA members, in preparation for upcoming events, such as masterclasses and concerts. Hold group meetings for important administrative matters that require a live assembly for discussion. On several occasions there are also masterclasses, concerts, studio recordings, etc., during which we head over to selected venues and carry out the activities.

Requirements

At least an ABRSM Grade 7 in Piano practical or in special cases when the student takes a different syllabus, he can approach Mr Tan Boon Beng to perform a piece of acceptable standard in an attempt to join the CCA.
