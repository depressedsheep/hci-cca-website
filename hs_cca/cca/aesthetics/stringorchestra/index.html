String Orchestra

Aesthetics

Back to Aesthetics

The Hwa Chong Institution String Orchestra, since its inception in the late 1980s, has grown from strength to strength, striving for excellence and greater heights. The orchestra has performed in many events and concerts, most recently Concord, a collaboration with the Australian Boys choir in 2007, Con Spirito, a joint effort with the Methodist Girls String Ensemble in 2007 and Ala Carte in 2006. In addition to the concerts and performances, the Hwa Chong Institution String Orchestra has also performed well in the Singapore youth festival central judging, clinching three consecutive golds in 2003, 2005, and most recently, in 2007. In the international music scene, the Hwa Chong Institution String Orchestra also emerged as first runner up in the open category of the 2006 International Gothenburg Music festival. Currently, string orchestra is led by Mr Chan Yoong Han, First Violinist (Fixed Chair) at the prestigious Singapore Symphony Orchestra.
