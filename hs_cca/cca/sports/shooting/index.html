Shooting

Sports

Back to Sports

Shooting Club has won many awards both nationally and internationally. We are a close group of people who share the same passion and strive together to achieve outstanding performance. Many of our shooters are selected into the National Youth Team (NYT) and the National Training Team (NTT). They represented Singapore in overseas competitions such as the South East Asia Shooting Championships (SEASA). We provide our shooters with the best equipment available so they can maximize their potential and hopefully represent Singapore in the Olympic Games someday. Although it is true that air rifle is not physically demanding, it requires the utmost determination and a strong passion to be able to emerge as champions. Do you have what it takes to be one?

What we do

For the first 30-40mins, we would be warming up and preparing

After which, we would shoot about 50 to 80 bullets, 50 for beginners and 80 for advanced shooters.

This is a constant battle of the mind.
