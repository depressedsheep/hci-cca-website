Wushu

Sports

Back to Sports

The Hwa Chong Wushu Club is a combination of a sports and performing arts CCA. As the name implies, our club focuses on Wushu which is an exhibition sport derived from traditional martial arts. The practice of Wushu involves martial art patterns, manoeuvres and sometimes the use of weapons. The forms in Wushu comprise basic movements based on traditional Chinese martial arts. In addition, the club also performs the 24 Festive Drums which is a fusion of Wushu and traditional Chinese drums. The 24 Festive Drums are often called upon to perform at important school events such as the Founders' Day and the welcoming ceremony of various ministers and guests-of-honour who grace our school events.
