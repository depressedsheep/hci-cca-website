Canoeing

Sports

Back to Sports

Canoeing is all about the need for speed! A fast and exhilarating sport which trains you both physically and mentally. A Hwa Chong canoeist is an all-rounder, doing very well in all aspects of school life, such as academics, sports and leadership. We have consistently produced many student leaders and scholars who are also medal winners. A common misconception is that being well-built is a prerequisite to be a canoeist. However, the truth is even the skinniest or the fattest person can be the one who holds numerous gold trophies as long as he shows commitment and sheer determination. Professional coaching and world-class equipment is provided free-of-charge. Last but not least, the Hwa Chong Canoeing team clinched the top three positions for both divisions for the past years, an academic achievement many sports CCAs envy. So if you crave for speed and want to develop your potential to the fullest, join canoeing!
