PE and CCA

National Cadet Corps

Uniform Groups

Back to Uniform Groups

With a blend of footdrills, armed rifle drills and precision drill training, coupled with our rigorous Physical Training framework and exposure to various adventure activities like mountaineering, HCINCC aims to bring out the disciplined, determined and dare-to-do spirit in you, instilling comradeship to enrich your learning and leadership process. Your life in NCC will be fraught with obstacles, yet these develop your adversity quotient, mental strength and capability to face reality, skills neglected in youths nowadays. The final product? The personal development and growth of each cadet through group dynamics and motivation. HCINCC abounds with accolades. The past 16 years of continuous Golds in the Best Unit Competition are testament to our success, and our Precision Drill Centre of Excellence initiative further propels us forward to scale greater heights. We are a trailblazing team of motivated people that have endured through blood, sweat and tears. Join the legacy: join HCINCC!
