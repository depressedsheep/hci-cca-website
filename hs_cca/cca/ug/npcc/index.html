The Hwa Chong Institution NPCC Unit was set up in 1966 and enjoys a long legacy of success, recognised as one of the top NPCC Units in Singapore. The unit has won the NPCC Inter-Unit Quiz Competition, a biennial competition organised by NPCC HQ for all school units, since 2001. In the annual NPCC Inter-Unit .22" Revolver Shooting Competition, the unit has also come in champions since 2009. Besides the core NPCC training syllabus, which includes drill, campcraft, survival skills and revolver shooting, the Hwa Chong NPCC Unit also introduces cadets to community service under the Community Awareness and Response Programme (CARP) and enrichment courses via the implementation of the Learning Experiences and Aspects Programme (LEAP), where cadets are given opportunities to participate in a wide variety of outdoor activities such as swimming and kayaking. Over the past few years, the unit has also been organising the Uniformed Groups Bowling Championships for cadets from all nine uniformed groups in Singapore. Through organising such major events, cadets are given the opportunity to develop their leadership skills, to learn to overcome adversity, as well as to hone their creative thinking ability.
