PE and CCA

Hwa Chong Institution PE and CCA Website

CCA: Choice defines education at Hwa Chong. We have 40 Co-curricular Activity groups for students to pick from. These include student-initiated interest groups to cater to our students' diverse passions and needs.

Sports for all: Hwa Chong encourages students and staff to lead a healthy lifestyle. We believe that by promoting sports for all, every member of the Hwa Chong Family will be healthy, happy and effective individuals.
